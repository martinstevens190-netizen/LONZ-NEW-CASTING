How casting works here

Tap the Cast icon to open the device chooser. For that chooser to appear and actually send the video, this app needs a real Google Cast receiver app ID and must be hosted on HTTPS.

Save your receiver app ID in the Cast setup area, then tap the Cast icon and pick a device. After the connection starts, the current video is sent automatically.

If no device list appears, the browser or device likely does not support Cast for web apps, or no Cast devices are discoverable on the network.
