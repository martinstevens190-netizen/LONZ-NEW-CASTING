How casting works here

Tap the Cast icon to open the device chooser. This web app works best in desktop Chrome on HTTPS.

Your receiver app ID is already prefilled. If your receiver is still unpublished, the TV or Chromecast must also be added as an authorized device in the Google Cast Developer Console and may need 5–15 minutes plus a reboot before testing.

This site now supports root receiver mode too, so the same Netlify root URL can act as the receiver when launched by Google Cast.
